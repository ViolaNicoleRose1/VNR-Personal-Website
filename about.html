About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate educator with six years of experience teaching in the Breathitt County School District. I teach art at Highland Turner Elementary School on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, and I am the library special class teacher at Breathitt Elementary School on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

I’m currently working toward my Master’s degree and Rank 1 certification in School Librarianship at Eastern Kentucky University. Education and lifelong learning are truly close to my heart.

Outside the classroom, I’m a proud mom to a wonderful 20-year-old daughter. I love spending time with family and friends, sharing lots of laughs along the way. Some of my favorite hobbies include reading, riding ATVs, swimming, going to the beach, and enjoying comedy shows. Life is better when you’re laughing and surrounded by good company!

Thanks for stopping by — I’m excited to share my journey and connect with you.

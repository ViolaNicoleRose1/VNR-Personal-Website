About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate educator with six years of experience teaching in the Breathitt County School District. I teach art at Highland Turner Elementary School and am working toward my Master’s in School Librarianship at Eastern Kentucky University.

I’m a proud mom and enjoy integrating creativity into literacy, riding ATVs, and making learning fun. Thank you for visiting—feel free to explore my experience or check out my photo gallery!
